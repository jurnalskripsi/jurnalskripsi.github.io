16. 3. My sister sweeps the floor every morning,The negative form of the sentence isa. My sister does sweep the floor every morningb. My sister does not sweep the floor every morningc. My sister does not sweeps the floor every morningd. My sister do not sweep the floor every morningbantu jawab yaa,,,Terima kasihz​


Jawaban:

3. My sister sweeps the floor every morning,

The negative form of the sentence is

b. My sister does not sweep the floor every morning

Penjelasan:

Materi : Simple Present Tense

Rumus:

+) S + V1 s/es + O/C

-) S + Do/Does + Not + V1 + O/ C

?) Do/does + S + V1 + O/C

The negative form of the sentence is “My sister does not sweep the floor every morning”

Jawabannya adalah B ✔️

PEMBAHASAN Present Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan Kegiatan yang dilakukan secara berulang, kebiasaan rutin atau fakta.

Rumusnya:

(+) S + V1 (-s/es)

(-) S + do/does + not + V1

(?) Do/does + S + V1?

Kalimat Present Tense

(+) My sister sweeps the floor every morning,

(-) My sister does not sweep the floor every morning ✔️

(?) Doed My sister sweep the floor every morning?
