12. carol has 7 more pens than twice the numby of pens gwen has. while lydia has 8 fewer pens than Carol. they have 66 pens in all how many pens does each girl have​


We use an analogy, Carol's pens as X, Gwen's pens as Y and Lydia's pens as Z.

So, the first sentence can be written as

X = 7 + 2Y

Second sentence can be written as

Z= X-8

Meanwhile, third sentence can be written as

X+Y+Z = 66

Next, we substitute 1st sentence to the 2nd, so it can be written as

Z = 7 + 2Y -8

Z= 2Y - 1

Therefore, we have all of the variables written equal to Y

So, we can sum all of them

7+ 2Y + Y + 2Y - 1 = 66

5Y = 60

Y = 12

So we now know that Gwen's pens are 12 pens. By substituting 12 as Y to the rest of the variables, we can conclude that

Carol= X = 7 + 12 x 2 = 31 pens

Lydia = X - 8 = 23 pens

Gwen = 12 pens
