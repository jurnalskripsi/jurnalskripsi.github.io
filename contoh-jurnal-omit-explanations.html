Explanation Text – Bulan Purnama (moon)

Full moon is the lunar phase that occurs when the moon is truly illuminated as seen from the earth. This can occur when the moon is in conflict with the sun (if it is already across the earth from the sun, more precisely, when the ecliptic of the sun and moon’s longitude is different from 180 degrees). This means that the hemisphere facing the earth (near side) is almost completely illuminated by sunlight and appears round (while the far side is almost completely unilluminated).

Lunar eclipses can occur only in full moons, where the moon’s orbit allows it to pass through the shadows of the earth. Lunar eclipses occur on a monthly basis because the moon usually passes above or below the shadows of the earth (which is largely restricted to ecliptic aircraft). Lunar eclipses can occur only when the full moon occurs in two orbital nodes, up or down nodes. This can cause an eclipse that only occurs every 6 months, and often 2 weeks before or after a solar eclipse on a new moon in the opposite Node.

Pembahasan

Benua Asia adalah benua terbesar di dunia dan paling padat penduduknya di dunia, terletak di bagian timur dan belahan utara. - Sebelah Utara berbatasan dengan Selat Bering dan Samudra Arktik

- Sebelah timur berbatasan dengan Samudra Pasifik

35. buatlah kalimat aktif dan pasif dari kata kerja omit menggunakan bahsa Inggris​


Jawaban:

Active: Our team chose to omit the project.
Passive: The project was omitted by our team.

Penjelasan (EN):

Sentences in Active Voice uses the Subject at the start of sentence. While as the Passive Voice uses Object at the start of a sentence. Passive Voice was invented to make sure that you claim a point, that you don't want to tell who actually did it. Notice when you try to comparate both active and passive voices, the meaning doesn't change. You can also add the author to whoever did it by using the phrase [by {Subject}.]. The easiest way to convert passive to active voice is to try to make a question about the statement. Does it make sense to turn it into passive? Both of them uses auxiliaries which is just the verb that is used to make a question about it. Once you found the auxiliaries, combine the remaining sentences and translate them into their other voices. Keep in mind that every Passive Voice uses Verb 3.

Penjelasan (ID):
Kalimat dalam Active Voice menggunakan Subjek di awal kalimat. Sedangkan sebagai Passive Voice menggunakan Objek di awal kalimat. Passive Voice diciptakan untuk memastikan bahwa Anda mengetahui suatu tujuan, bahwa Anda tidak ingin memberi tahu siapa yang sebenarnya melakukannya. Perhatikan ketika Anda mencoba membandingkan Active dan Passive Voice, artinya tidak berubah. Anda juga dapat menambahkan penulis ke siapa pun yang melakukannya dengan menggunakan frasa [oleh {Subjek}.]. Cara termudah untuk mengubah Passive Voice menjadi Active Voice adalah dengan mencoba membuat pertanyaan tentang pernyataan tersebut. Apakah kalimat tersebut masuk akal untuk mengubahnya menjadi Passive Voice? Keduanya menggunakan kata bantu yang hanya kata kerja yang digunakan untuk membuat pertanyaan tentang itu. Setelah Anda menemukan kata bantu, gabungkan kalimat yang tersisa dan terjemahkan ke dalam kalimat yang lain. Ingat bahwa semua kalimat Passive Voice berupa kata kerja ketiga.


36. Teks explanations Life cycle of any animals


Animals that Grow Up (Simple Life Cycle):

Most animals including fish, mammals, reptiles and birds have very simple life cycles:

they are born (either alive from their mother or hatched from eggs)
they grow up
These animals have three stages -- before birth, young and adult. The young are typically similar to the parent, just smaller. The young slowly "grow" to become adults.

39. explanations, sentence examples, sentence patterns, and functions of infinitve


Explanation : The infinitive is the base form of a verb. In English, when we talk about the infinitive we are usually referring to the present infinitive, which is the most common. There are, however, four other forms of the infinititive: the perfect infinitive, the perfect continuous infinitive, the continuous infinitive, & the passive infinitive.
Examples : She came to collect her pay cheque.
The three bears went to find firewood.
I am calling to ask you about dad.
Patterns : Subject + to be + noun phrase + to-infinitive
Function : An infinitive can constitute the subject of a sentence.
