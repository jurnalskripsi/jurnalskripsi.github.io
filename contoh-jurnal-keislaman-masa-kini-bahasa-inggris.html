3. Contoh Surat untuk Majalah tentang Masalah Kita dalam bahasa Inggris


Artinya
Kumpulan Contoh Berita Dalam Bahasa Inggris dan Artinya Terbaru
The Jakarta Post – Mitsubishi Motors Corp., the Japanese automaker tarnished by a massive recall-cover-up 15 years ago, owned up to another scandal Wednesday, saying employees had intentionally falsified fuel mileage test data for several vehicle models.
The inaccurate tests by the Tokyo-based automaker involved 157,000 of its own-brand eK wagon and eK Space light passenger cars, and 468,000 Dayz and Dayz Roox vehicles produced for Nissan Motor Co.
The models are all so-called “minicars” with tiny engines whose main attraction is generally great mileage. They were produced from March 2013.
The problem surfaced after Nissan pointed out inconsistencies in data, the company said. Mitsubishi conducted an internal probe and found that tire pressure data was falsified to make mileage appear better than it actually was.
“The wrong doing was intentional. It is clear the falsification was done to make the mileage look better. But why they would resort to fraud to do this is still unclear,” company president Tetsuro Aikawa told reporters. He and other company executives bowed in apology.
Artinya:
The Jakarta Post – Perusahaan Mitsubishi Motors, produsen mobil Jepang ternodai oleh recall-cover-up besar-besaran 15 tahun yang lalu, mengakui sejujurnya pebuatan memalukan yang lainnya pada hari Rabu, mengatakan para karyawan telah dengan sengaja memalsukan data jarak tempuh uji bahan bakar untuk beberapa model kendaraan.
Tes yang tidak akurat oleh perusahaan mobil yang berbasis di Tokyo tersebut melibatkan 157.000 mobil merek mera sendiri seperti eK wagon dan eK Space light mobil penumpang, dan 468.000 Dayz dan kendaraan Dayz Roox diproduksi untuk Perusahaan Nissan Motor.

4. contoh pengalaman di masa lampau dalam bahasa inggris


Contoh recount text pengalaman masa lalu:

A few weeks ago, I went to Gemah beach with my friends. It is located in Tulungagung city, East Java. The trip there took about 1,5 hours. We left from my home at 07.00 using some cars and we arrived about 08.30 o’clock.

As arrived in the Gemah beach, one of my friend rented a hut to take a rest. I and my friends immediately ran towards to the beach. It looked sunny day with the blue sky and the sun clearly but I felt a gentle breeze. The waves there were suitable for swimming, but unfortunately I couldn’t swim. I just played with white sand and built a sand castle with some my friends. I also take a selfie with the background of  beautiful beach.

The day had begun in the evening. We hurried to go home. Before that we went to home, I bought some souvenirs in there. Finally, we arrived at 6:00 PM because the trip was over.

Pembahasan

Recount text dalam bahasa inggris artinya salah satu dari jenis teks yang berisi cerita pengalaman kita di masa yang lampau. Tujuan dari Recount Text biasanya untuk menceritakan kembali dan memberikan informasi serta menghibur para pembaca.

Karena cerita-cerita yang dituliskan pada recount text adalah hal yang terjadi di masa lampau, maka tenses (bentuk waktu) yang kita gunakan adalah Simple Past Tense. Selain itu, terdapat juga penggunaan adverbial phrase atau yang bisa kita sebut sebagai keterangan tempat dan waktu.

Pembahasan

Contoh Abstract Jurnal Bahasa Inggris:



Improving Students’ Achievement In Writing Analytical Exposition Paragraph Through Debate Technique


ABSTRACT


The objective of this research is to find out the effectiveness of the implementation of debate technique in order to improve students‟ achievement in writing analytical exposition paragraph. The study was conducted by using class room action research. The subject of this study was the second year students of SMA Santo Thomas 4 Binjai consisting of 25 students.

The study was implemented in two cycles and each cycle consisted of three meetings. The instruments of data collection were writing test and social judgement (diary notes, observation sheets, questionnaire, and interview). The research result in that 1) the implementation of debate technique is effective to improve students‟ achievement in writing analytical exposition paragraph which is in writing test result, students‟ scores kept improving in every test that In orientation the mean score was 28,8, in the test of cycle I 51,8 and in the test of second cycle was 65. It means there is a significant improvement toward the before, after, during several times of the aplication, 2) students‟ perception on debate technique is good and positive, 3) and the teaching – learning process run well That students were active and enthusiastic in writing.

Key Word: Debate, Analytical Exposition, Improving

Paragraph 1:

Back in the summer of 1995, when I was just a young teenager, my family embarked on a memorable road trip across the country. We packed our bags, loaded up the old station wagon, and hit the open road. Our destination was the Grand Canyon, a place of wonder and natural beauty that I had only seen in pictures. As we drove through the vast landscapes, I remember being in awe of the changing scenery and the vastness of the country. It was a journey filled with laughter, sing-alongs, and the excitement of exploring new places.

Paragraph 2:

On our journey, we made several stops along the way to immerse ourselves in the local culture and attractions. One of the most memorable stops was in Nashville, Tennessee, where we got to experience the vibrant music scene and visit the Country Music Hall of Fame. The streets were bustling with musicians, and we even had the chance to listen to live performances in some of the famous honky-tonk bars. It was a magical experience, and I still remember the melodies and the warmth of the people we encountered.

Finally, we arrived at the Grand Canyon, and words cannot describe the awe and majesty of that place. Standing on the edge of the canyon, I felt insignificant in the face of such natural beauty. The colors of the rocks, the deep canyons, and the vastness of the landscape left an indelible impression on me. We hiked down into the canyon, camped under the stars, and enjoyed breathtaking sunsets. It was a once-in-a-lifetime experience that I cherish to this day.

Looking back, that road trip was not just a vacation, but a journey of self-discovery and creating lifelong memories. The sights, the sounds, and the shared moments with my family are etched in my heart forever. It was a trip down memory lane that shaped my love for travel, adventure, and the beauty of the world around us.

37. contoh cerita pengalaman masa kecil dalam bahasa inggris​


Berikut ini adalah pengalamanku berpergian bersama ayahku!

Last holiday, my father and I went to Jakarta. I was very happy. We went to Jakarta by plane. It was my first experience travelled by plane.

On the first day we arrived, we stayed at a hotel. The hotel was very beautiful and clean. We just stayed 3 days in Jakarta. The next day my father took me to the zoo and old city. In the zoo there were so many animals like elephants, snakes, crocodiles, lions, birds, etc. On the last day, we visited my father's friend in Bogor. We went there by car.

It was my great experience with my father. I hope I can travel with my family on the next holiday.

Artinya:

Liburan lalu, ayahku dan saya pergi ke Jakarta. Saya sangat senang. Kami pergi ke Jakarta menggunakan pesawat. Itu adalah pengalaman pertamaku menaiki pesawat.

Hari pertama tiba, kami menginap di salah satu hotel. Hotelnya sangat bersih dan indah. Kami hanya tinggal di sana selama 3 hari. Pada hari berikutnya ayahku mengajakku ke kebun binatang dan kota tua. Di kebun binatang ada banyak hewan seperti gajah, ular, buaya, singa burung dan lain-lain. Pada hari terakhir, kami mengunjungi teman ayahku di Bogor. Kami pergi ke sana menggunakan mobil.

Itu adalah pengalaman berhargaku dengan ayah. Saya harap saya bisa berpergian bersama keluargaku di liburan berikutnya.

Pembahasan

Menceritakan pengalaman merupakan contoh dari recount text. Struktur recount text adalah:

Orientation merupakan pengenalan. Penulis mengenalkan pengalaman apa yang akan dia ceritakan.Event merupakan kejadian-kejadian yang terjadi.Re-orientation merupakan penutup. Biasanya penulis menuliskan pesan dan kesan dari pengalamannya tersebut.

Karena menceritakan kejadian di masa lampau, maka tenses yang digunakan adalah simple past tense. Simple past tense menggunakan kata kerja bentuk ke2. Contohnya I went by plane. Kata kerja pergi "go" diubah menjadi bentuk kedua yaitu "went".
