4. mention and explain the generic structures of discussion text​


Jawaban:

The generic structure of a discussion text usually consists of three main parts: introduction, body, and conclusion. Here's an explanation of each part:

Introduction: This is where the topic is introduced and the purpose of the discussion is stated. The introduction should be clear, concise, and engaging to capture the reader's attention. It often begins with a general statement about the topic and then narrows down to the specific issue being discussed. The last sentence of the introduction is usually the thesis statement, which presents the writer's argument or point of view.

Body: This is the main part of the discussion text and contains several paragraphs. Each paragraph presents a different aspect or point of view related to the topic. The writer must provide supporting evidence for each point made to persuade the reader to agree with their argument. The body should be well-organized, with each paragraph focusing on a single idea and smoothly transitioning to the next.

Conclusion: This is where the writer summarizes their argument and presents a concluding statement. The conclusion should restate the thesis statement in different words and summarize the main points of the discussion. The writer should also provide a final statement that leaves a lasting impression on the reader. It can be a call to action, a prediction, or a suggestion for further discussion.

In summary, the introduction provides the context and thesis statement, the body presents the supporting evidence, and the conclusion summarizes the argument and presents a concluding statement.

Tiara : A. You can read the books and use the computers

Pembahasan:

A. You can read the books and use the computers  

Translate: Kamu bisa membaca buku dan menggunakan komputer

Merupakan kalimat tipe present tense; sesuai dengan apa yang ditanyakan Andi.  Sebaiknya kita hanya menggunakan perpustakaan untuk hal-hal yang berhubungan dengan pembelajaran kita di sekolah dan pekerjaan yang kita lakukan tidak mengganggu suasana tenang yang seharusnya tercipta di perpustakaan. B. You must read the books and watch films only  

Translate: Kamu harus membaca buku dan menonton film

Merupakan kalimat tipe English modal verb ‘must’; seakan-akan Tiara meminta Andi untuk membaca dan menonton film di dalam perpustakan, tidak sesuai dengan apa yang ditanyakan oleh Andi.

C. You can read books, watch films, and use the computer

Translate: Kamu bisa membaca buku, menonton film, dan menggunakan komputer

Merupakan kalimat tipe present tense; tidak sesuai dengan fungsi perpusatakan pada umumnya, jarang ditemui perpustakan yang memperbolehkan untuk menonton film, karena dinilai dapat mengganggu pengunjung perpustakan yang sedang berkonsentrasi di sana.

D. You must not use the computers, but you can read the books

Translate: Kamu tidak boleh menggunakan komputer, tapi kamu bisa membaca buku

Merupakan kalimat tipe English modal verb ‘must’; Tiara memerintahkan Andi untuk tidak menggunakan komputer, namun memperbolehkannya untuk membaca buku.

32. mention and explain the generic structures of discussion text​


Jawaban:

A discussion text is a type of text that presents an issue or a problem and discusses different viewpoints or arguments about it. The purpose of a discussion text is to present different perspectives and arguments in a logical and organized way, and to provide a conclusion or recommendation based on the arguments presented. The generic structures of a discussion text typically consist of three main parts:

Introduction: This part introduces the topic and provides background information about the issue being discussed. It also presents the thesis statement, which is the main argument or position that the writer will take in the discussion.

Body: This part presents the main arguments or points for and against the issue being discussed. Each argument or point is typically presented in a separate paragraph, and is supported by evidence or examples. The writer may also provide counterarguments to address opposing viewpoints.

Conclusion: This part summarizes the main points discussed in the body, restates the thesis statement, and provides a final recommendation or conclusion based on the arguments presented. The conclusion may also include a call to action or a suggestion for further discussion or research.

Overall, the goal of a discussion text is to provide a balanced and objective analysis of an issue, and to encourage readers to think critically and form their own opinions based on the arguments presented.
